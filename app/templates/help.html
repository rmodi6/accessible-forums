Welcome to the Accessibility Blog

Use the following NVDA screen reader keyboard shortcuts to navigate through the website:

  • F to navigate to the search field form
  • H to navigate the headings on the page.
  • K to navigate the links on a page. Thread view has links to thread on the page while posts view has links to go back to thread and open parent, sibling and child posts.
  • T to navigate tables. All the posts are in a table layout for easy navigation. Use Ctrl+Alt+ ↑/↓ to navigate between posts.
